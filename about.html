About LegalFlow

LegalFlow is your smart assistant for legal document sentiment analysis, crafted by Synergy Spheres 3.0. It empowers lawyers, researchers, and legal tech innovators to explore emotional cues within legal documents — from contracts to court opinions.

Built with cutting-edge AI tools and a sleek user interface, LegalFlow offers real-time document uploads, sentiment insights, and multilingual dashboards. Whether you're reviewing breach of contract cases or client communications, our system delivers precision and clarity.

This project is open-source and continuously evolving. Feel free to contribute, customize, and integrate LegalFlow into your legal-tech workflows.
